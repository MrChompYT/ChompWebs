Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Basic websites typically take 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects can take 3-4 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! Every package includes a warranty period (1-3 months depending on the package). After that, I offer ongoing support at $25 per change.

Can you work with my existing brand?

Absolutely! I can work with your existing brand guidelines, colors, and logos to create a cohesive website that matches your brand identity.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

Changes during the warranty period are free. After that, I charge $25 per change, ensuring your website stays current and functional.
